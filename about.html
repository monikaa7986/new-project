About the Language Translation System

Our Language Translation System aims to bridge the gap between different regional languages in India and English. This system provides an automated way to translate text from English to various Indian languages with high accuracy and minimal human involvement for proof-reading.

The system uses advanced AI algorithms to ensure smooth translation, making it highly efficient and accessible for educational institutions, businesses, and government agencies.
